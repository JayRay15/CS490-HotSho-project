# Mentor Collaboration Feature - Quick Start Guide

## üéØ Overview

The Mentor Collaboration feature allows job seekers to:
- Invite mentors and career coaches
- Share progress and application materials
- Receive targeted feedback
- Track recommended actions
- Communicate securely with mentors
- Generate progress reports

## üöÄ Getting Started

### For Mentees (Job Seekers)

#### Step 1: Invite a Mentor
1. Navigate to **Mentor Hub** (in main navigation)
2. Click **"+ Invite Mentor"** button
3. Enter mentor's email address
4. Select mentorship type: Mentor, Career Coach, or Peer Mentor
5. Choose focus areas (resume, interviews, job strategy, etc.)
6. Select what data to share (resume, cover letters, goals, etc.)
7. Add a personal message (optional but recommended)
8. Click **"Send Invitation"**

**What happens next:**
- Invitation email sent to mentor
- Mentor can accept/decline from email link
- Once accepted, relationship shows in "My Mentors"

#### Step 2: Share Progress
1. Go to **Progress Sharing** section
2. Select a connected mentor
3. Click **"Generate Report"**
4. Choose report type: Weekly, Monthly, or Custom
5. Report auto-generates with your job search metrics
6. Mentor receives notification to review

#### Step 3: Receive Feedback
1. Check **Feedback** tab for mentor's comments
2. View suggestions and ratings
3. Click **"Acknowledge"** to mark as reviewed
4. Use feedback to improve resume, interview prep, etc.

#### Step 4: Implement Recommendations
1. Check **Recommendations** tab
2. See list of suggested actions with priority levels
3. Move recommendation to "In Progress" as you work on it
4. Add progress notes to show what you've done
5. Mark as "Completed" when finished
6. Mentor gets update on your implementation

#### Step 5: Communicate
1. Go to **Messages** section
2. Select a mentor from the list
3. Send questions or updates
4. Mentor receives message notification
5. Have real-time conversation about your job search

### For Mentors/Career Coaches

#### Step 1: Accept Invitation
1. Receive email invitation from mentee
2. Click **"Accept Invitation"** or sign up if new to HotSho
3. Access mentee's dashboard in **My Mentees** section

#### Step 2: Review Mentee Progress
1. Go to **My Mentees** in Mentor Hub
2. Click on a mentee to view their profile
3. See shared materials: resume, applications, goals
4. Review job search metrics and progress

#### Step 3: Provide Feedback
1. Review mentee's resume, cover letters, or interview prep
2. In dashboard, click **"Add Feedback"**
3. Select what you're providing feedback on
4. Rate (1-5 stars) if applicable
5. Write detailed feedback
6. Add specific suggestions with priorities
7. Submit - mentee gets notification

#### Step 4: Create Recommendations
1. Identify action items for mentee
2. Click **"Add Recommendation"**
3. Title: Clear action (e.g., "Update resume summary")
4. Description: Detailed guidance
5. Category: Type of recommendation
6. Priority: High, Medium, or Low
7. Target date: When you expect completion
8. Submit - mentee sees in their recommendations

#### Step 5: Monitor Progress
1. Check **My Mentees** for status updates
2. View mentee's progress on recommendations
3. Read progress notes they add
4. Monitor monthly progress reports
5. See recommendation completion status

#### Step 6: Stay Connected
1. Go to **Messages** with mentee
2. Send encouragement and guidance
3. Answer questions about job search
4. Celebrate wins and milestones
5. Help troubleshoot challenges

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Can I invite multiple mentors?**
A: Yes! Different mentors can have different specialties (e.g., resume expert, interview coach, industry mentor).

**Q: What if my mentor doesn't have a HotSho account?**
A: They'll get an email with a link to sign up and accept your invitation.

**Q: Can I cancel a mentorship?**
A: Yes, go to the mentor's profile and click "Cancel Mentorship". You can reconnect later.

**Q: How often should I share progress?**
A: We recommend monthly reports, but you can generate anytime. Discuss frequency with your mentor.

**Q: Are messages private?**
A: Yes, messages are only visible to you and your mentor in your relationship.

**Q: Can I share less information if I want privacy?**
A: Absolutely! Before sending invitation, uncheck any data categories you don't want to share.

**Q: What if I disagree with feedback?**
A: You don't have to implement everything. Acknowledge it and discuss with your mentor if needed.

**Q: Can mentors see my other mentors?**
A: No, each mentorship is private. Mentors can only see your shared profile data.
