# UC-095: Professional Reference Management - User Guide

## How to Access the Features

### Step 1: Navigate to the Network Page
1. Log in to your account
2. Click on **"Network"** in the main navigation menu (top navigation bar)

### Step 2: Access the References Tab
Once on the Network page, you'll see a tab navigation bar with the following tabs:
- **My Contacts** (default view)
- **References** â† Click here for reference management
- **Referrals**
- **Events**

Click on the **"References"** tab to access all reference management features.

---

## Feature Overview

### 1. Add a Reference
**Location:** References tab > "Add Reference" button (top right)

**Steps:**
1. Click the **"Add Reference"** button
2. A modal will appear showing all your existing contacts
3. Click on any contact to designate them as a professional reference
4. They will now appear in your References list

**Note:** You can only add contacts that already exist in your network. If you need to add someone new, first add them as a contact in the "My Contacts" tab.

---

### 2. Request a Reference (AI-Generated Email)
**Location:** References tab > Any reference card > "Request Reference" button

**Steps:**
1. Find the reference you want to request
2. Click the **"Request Reference"** button on their card
3. In the modal that appears:
   - Select the **job application** you're requesting the reference for (dropdown menu)
   - Click **"Generate Request"**
4. The AI will generate a personalized email including:
   - **Email Subject** (professional and appropriate)
   - **Email Body** (personalized based on your relationship and the job)
   - **Talking Points** (specific achievements they can mention)
   - **Preparation Tips** (guidance for your reference)
   - **Timeline** (when they might be contacted)
5. Click **"Copy Email"** to copy the entire email to your clipboard
6. Paste it into your email client and send it to your reference

**AI Features:**
- The email is customized based on:
  - Your relationship type (Mentor, Manager, Colleague, etc.)
  - Relationship strength (Strong, Medium, Weak, New)
  - Last contact date
  - The specific job you're applying for
  - Your work history and skills

---

### 3. Generate a Reference Portfolio (Printable Document)
**Location:** References tab > Select references > "Generate Portfolio" button

**Steps:**
1. In the References tab, you'll see a **checkbox** in the top-left corner of each reference card
2. **Check the boxes** next to the references you want to include in your portfolio
3. A button will appear at the top: **"Generate Portfolio (X)"** where X is the number selected
4. Click this button
5. A professional document will appear showing:
   - All selected references with their details
   - Contact information (email, phone, LinkedIn)
   - Relationship type and strength
   - Professional roles and companies
   - Any notes you've added

**Actions Available:**
- **"Print / Save as PDF"** - Opens the print dialog to save as PDF or print
- **"Copy as Text"** - Copies all reference information as plain text to clipboard

**Use Cases:**
- Attach to job applications that require a reference list
- Include in application packages
- Share with recruiters or hiring managers
- Keep for your records

---

### 4. Remove a Reference
**Location:** References tab > Any reference card > "Remove" button

**Steps:**
1. Find the reference you want to remove
2. Click the **"Remove"** button (red text)
3. They will be removed from your references list but remain in your contacts

**Note:** This doesn't delete the contact, it just removes their "reference" designation.

## Tips for Best Results

### When Adding References
1. **Choose Strategically:** Select people who:
   - Know your work well
   - Can speak to relevant skills for your target roles
   - Have worked with you recently (within 2-3 years ideal)
   - Have agreed to be your reference

2. **Keep Information Updated:** Ensure their contact details are current before generating a portfolio

### When Requesting References
1. **Select the Right Job:** Choose the specific application so the AI can customize the message
2. **Review Before Sending:** Always read the generated email and adjust if needed
3. **Provide Context:** The talking points help your reference prepare, so share them
4. **Follow Up:** After sending, track whether they respond and confirm

### For Reference Portfolios
1. **Select Relevant References:** Include 3-5 references (standard for most applications)
2. **Vary Reference Types:** Mix supervisors, colleagues, and mentors if possible
3. **Update Regularly:** Regenerate your portfolio before each major application wave

---

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** "Add Reference" button doesn't show any contacts
- **Solution:** First add contacts in the "My Contacts" tab. You need existing contacts to designate as references.

**Problem:** AI email generation fails
- **Solution:** Ensure you have selected a job application and your reference has basic information filled out.

**Problem:** Portfolio doesn't display correctly
- **Solution:** Ensure your browser allows pop-ups and printing. Try using Chrome or Firefox for best results.

**Problem:** Can't find the References tab
- **Solution:** Make sure you're on the Network page (check the URL or navigation menu). The tab bar is just below the statistics cards.
