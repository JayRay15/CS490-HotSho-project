# Cover Letter Tone & Style Adjustment - Quick Start Guide

## Overview
Customize your AI-generated cover letters to match different company cultures, industries, and your personal style preferences.

## How to Use

### Step 1: Create a Cover Letter
1. Navigate to the **Cover Letters** section
2. Click **"Add Cover Letter"**
3. Select **"AI Generate"**

### Step 2: Select Your Job
- Choose from your saved jobs
- **Auto-magic**: Settings are automatically recommended based on job details!

### Step 3: Choose Your Tone ‚≠ê
Pick the tone that matches your style and the company:

| Tone | Best For | Example |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Formal** | Traditional companies, finance, legal | "I am writing to express my interest..." |
| **Casual** | Startups, creative agencies, tech | "I'm excited to apply for..." |
| **Enthusiastic** | Sales, marketing, startup roles | "I'm thrilled about the opportunity..." |
| **Analytical** | Data roles, research, consulting | "My 40% efficiency improvement demonstrates..." |
| **Creative** | Design, content, creative roles | "Imagine a team member who..." |
| **Technical** | Engineering, IT, development | "My expertise with microservices architecture..." |
| **Executive** | Leadership, C-suite, director roles | "Throughout my career leading strategic initiatives..." |

### Step 4: Set Industry & Culture
- **Industry**: Select your target industry (auto-detected from job)
- **Company Culture**: Choose the company type
  - Startup (fast-paced, innovative)
  - Corporate (structured, established)
  - Enterprise (large-scale, complex)
  - Agency (client-focused, projects)
  - Nonprofit (mission-driven)
  - Remote-First (distributed team)

### Step 5: Advanced Options (Optional) üé®
Click **"Show Advanced Options"** for more control:

#### Length Options:
- **Brief** (250-300 words): Quick and concise
- **Standard** (300-400 words): Balanced ‚≠ê Recommended
- **Detailed** (400-500 words): Comprehensive

#### Writing Style:
- **Direct**: Straightforward, action-oriented
- **Narrative**: Story-driven, shows career journey
- **Hybrid**: Best of both ‚≠ê Recommended

#### Custom Instructions:
Add specific points you want emphasized (up to 500 characters)

Example:
```
"Emphasize my remote work experience and mention my contribution to 
the open-source community. Highlight my leadership in agile transformation."
```

#### Number of Variations:
Generate 1-3 different versions to choose from

The system will alert you if settings might conflict:
- ‚ú® "Tip: Startup culture often prefers enthusiastic tone"
- ‚ö†Ô∏è "Note: Finance industry expects formal communication"

### Step 7: Generate! üöÄ
Click **"Generate Cover Letter"** and wait (can take up to 60 seconds for multiple variations)

## Tips for Best Results

### üéØ Tone Selection Tips
- **Match the company**: Research their culture before selecting
- **When in doubt**: Use Formal for conservative industries, Casual for tech/startups
- **Multiple applications**: Generate variations to compare

### üè¢ Industry Selection Tips
- **Be specific**: Don't use "General" if you can be more specific
- **Keywords matter**: Industry selection adds relevant terminology
- Technology ‚Üí API, scalability, agile
- Finance ‚Üí ROI, compliance, risk management
- Healthcare ‚Üí HIPAA, patient care, clinical

### üé® Culture Selection Tips
- **Read job descriptions carefully**: Look for cultural clues
  - "Fast-paced startup" ‚Üí Startup
  - "Established Fortune 500" ‚Üí Corporate/Enterprise
  - "Remote-first team" ‚Üí Remote-First
  - "Make a difference" ‚Üí Nonprofit

### ‚úçÔ∏è Custom Instructions Tips
- **Be specific**: "Mention AWS certification" vs "Good tech skills"
- **Prioritize**: List most important points first
- **Stay focused**: Keep it relevant to the job
- **Use keywords**: Include terms from job description

### üîÑ Variation Tips
- **Try different tones**: Generate 3 variations with different tones
- **Compare results**: See which resonates best
- **Iterate**: Adjust settings and regenerate if needed

## Troubleshooting

### "Settings don't match my job"
- Override auto-recommendations manually
- Click into each dropdown to select your preference

### "Cover letter feels too formal/casual"
- Try a different tone
- Use "Hybrid" writing style for balance
- Add custom instructions for specific adjustments

### "Not enough industry-specific terms"
- Ensure correct industry is selected
- Add key terms in custom instructions
- Use "Technical" or "Analytical" tone for more terminology

### "Too long/short"
- Adjust the length setting
- Brief: Under 300 words
- Standard: 300-400 words
- Detailed: 400-500 words

### "Doesn't show my personality"
- Try "Creative" or "Enthusiastic" tone
- Use "Narrative" writing style
- Add personality points in custom instructions

## FAQ

**Q: Can I save my preferred settings?**
A: Settings are auto-recommended per job. You can reuse the same selections for similar jobs.

**Q: How do variations differ?**
A: Each variation emphasizes different aspects of your background using the same tone and settings.

**Q: Can I edit after generation?**
A: Yes! Save the generated letter and edit it in the cover letter editor.

**Q: Will this work for any job?**
A: Yes, but results are best when you have:
- Complete work experience in your profile
- Saved job with detailed description
- Appropriate settings for the target company

**Q: How long does generation take?**
A: 15-60 seconds depending on:
- Number of variations (1-3)
- Complexity of job description
- Length setting

**Q: What if auto-recommendations seem wrong?**
A: Override them! The system makes educated guesses, but you know the company best.
